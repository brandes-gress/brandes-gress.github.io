Kanban vs. Scrum vs. Waterfall
Kanban

Visualizes work as a continuous flow, limits what's in progress at any time, and lets teams pull new tasks only when there's capacity to handle them.

Scrum

Breaks work into fixed sprints with defined goals, daily check-ins, and structured reviews so teams ship in predictable cycles.

Waterfall

Moves through each phase in sequence, from requirements to design to build to test to launch, with a clear handoff between every stage.
